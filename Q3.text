Q3. Difference between unary and binary operators in javascript. Give examples of each

    solution - operators in javascript are categorized based on the numbers of operands they require, into the following groups

    . unary operators :- unary operators operate on single operands
    . Binary operators :- Binary operators operate on two operands

    1] unary operators : (-), (+), (--), (++),(!)

    2]Binary operators : (+), (-), (*), (==), (===), (!=), (!==), (<), (>), (<=), (>=),(&&), (||)
